Who built this, and why

This is not another 4% rule blog.

ETQ was built by a career financial professional who retired at 53 and now lives a rich, unhurried life using a sophisticated Excel version of this very tool — the one he used on himself.

That distinction matters. Most of what is written about early retirement is produced by people still working, still selling, still needing an audience. This tool carries the perspective of someone on the other side of the question — who did the calculation honestly, trusted the number, and walked.

It is designed for professionals who are tired of being patronised with rules of thumb, who want a proper projection to end of life, and who would like to stop asking the question and simply know the answer.
